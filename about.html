Who We Are

Total Performance Manpower Supply Corp. is a Department of Migrant Workers (DMW) accredited recruitment agency committed to ethical hiring practices and quality workforce deployment.

With years of experience in the industry, we've built strong partnerships with employers worldwide while ensuring the welfare and rights of our deployed workers. Our team consists of seasoned professionals who understand the complexities of international recruitment and are dedicated to providing exceptional service to both employers and job seekers.

We specialize in connecting skilled Filipino professionals with reputable employers across various industries including healthcare, construction, hospitality, and engineering.

Our Mission

The company aims to provide skilled Filipino workers to partner companies, focusing on service excellence. They seek decent abroad employment opportunities and aim to expand job and profession opportunities while improving employee capabilities to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

We are world-class Company that excels in best quality recruitment and reliable services delivery to partner-companies and worker-applicants.

Our Core Values

Integrity
