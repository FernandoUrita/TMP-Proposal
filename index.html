Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about overseas employment

What documents do I need to apply for overseas jobs?

Typically, you'll need the following documents: updated resume, passport, diploma/TOR, employment certificates, PRC ID (if applicable), NBI clearance, and other certificates relevant to your profession. Specific requirements may vary by country and position.

Are there any placement fees?

As a DMW-accredited agency, we strictly comply with government regulations regarding fees. There are no placement fees charged to applicants. Only legitimate processing fees (visa, medical, etc.) may apply, which will be clearly explained during orientation.

How long does the recruitment process take?

The timeline varies depending on the position and destination country. Typically, the process takes 2-4 months from application to deployment. Some specialized positions or countries with stricter requirements may take longer.

What support do you provide after deployment?

We provide continuous support through our overseas partners and our 24/7 helpline. We assist with contract concerns, workplace issues, and any emergencies. We also conduct regular check-ins during your employment period.
