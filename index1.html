Registered Nurses

Saudi Arabia, UAE, UK
×

Job Description

We are seeking qualified Registered Nurses to join our healthcare teams in various international locations. You will be responsible for providing high-quality patient care, administering medications, monitoring patient progress, and collaborating with healthcare teams to ensure optimal patient outcomes.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Nursing from a recognized institution
  • Minimum 2 years of hospital experience (government hospital experience preferred)
  • Valid PRC license and updated board certification
  • BLS/ACLS certification (preferred but not required)
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to adapt to different healthcare systems and cultures

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package ($1,200 - $2,500/month depending on location and experience)
  • Free accommodation or housing allowance
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Annual paid vacation with round-trip airfare
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Career advancement possibilities

Additional Information

  • Contract duration: 2 years (renewable)
  • Work schedule: 48 hours per week (8-hour shifts)
  • Overtime compensation available
  • Pre-departure orientation and training provided
  • Airport pickup and settling-in assistance
